Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Lesktop ("we") processes personal data in connection with website operation and provision of cleaning services.

Data Controller

Lesktop, Prague, Czech Republic
Email: team@lesktop.cz
Phone: +420 775 344 145

What Data We Process

  • Identification and contact data: name, email, phone.
  • Request data: cleaning type, area, frequency, additional services, notes.
  • Technical data: IP address and similar data necessary for website operation.

Purposes and Legal Basis

  • Processing requests and communication: contract performance or negotiation (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Records and service improvement: legitimate interest in operation and development (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Legal obligations (e.g., accounting): Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR.

Who We Share Data With

For form submissions, we use Web3Forms service (email delivery intermediary). IT service providers may have access to data only to the necessary extent and based on contractual safeguards.

Retention Period

  • Requests and communication: usually up to 24 months from resolution.
  • Accounting documents: according to legal deadlines (5–10 years).

Cookies and Analytics

To continuously improve our services, website and advertising campaigns, we use tools from Google Ireland Limited:

  • Google Search Console – anonymous tracking of website performance in search.
  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) – anonymized data on visits (device type, visit duration, website behavior).
  • Google Ads – measuring advertising campaign effectiveness, conversions and related cookies (e.g., for remarketing), always only with consent.

Your consent: Google Analytics and Google Ads are activated only after clicking "I Agree" in the cookie bar. Without consent, these scripts are not loaded and no analytical or advertising cookies are stored.

Option to refuse: If you choose "Necessary Only", no analytical or advertising cookies will be stored and your visit will not be recorded in GA or Google Ads. The website remains fully functional.

Your Rights

  • Right to access, correction, deletion and restriction of processing.
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to data portability.
  • Right to file a complaint with the Office for Personal Data Protection (www.uoou.cz).

Contact

To exercise rights or ask questions, write to team@lesktop.cz.
