Home Cleaning in Prague

We offer professional cleaning services for homes and businesses in Prague and Central Bohemia. Regular cleaning, deep cleaning, post-renovation cleaning, and office cleaning. Price depends on area and type of cleaning – below you'll find an overview of how much apartment cleaning costs and how to order.

What We Clean

  • Regular home cleaning – vacuuming, floor washing, surface wiping, kitchen, bathroom, toilet, trash removal. Frequency by agreement (weekly, every 14 days, monthly).
  • Deep cleaning – more thorough cleaning including hard-to-reach areas, detailed bathroom and kitchen, window and frame washing by agreement.
  • Post-renovation or moving cleaning – removal of dust and construction debris, preparation of space for normal use.
  • Office and commercial space cleaning – offices, shops, common areas. Regular care and one-time jobs.
  • Window washing and additional services – window washing, ironing, carpet cleaning by individual agreement.

We operate throughout Prague (all districts) and by agreement also in the surrounding area – Prague-west, Prague-east, Central Bohemian Region.

How Much It Costs

Cleaning price depends on area (m²), type of cleaning (regular / one-time / deep) and any additional services. Final price is always agreed in advance.

  • Regular home cleaning: from 800 CZK (up to 35 m²) to approximately 3,000 CZK (131–150 m²). One-time cleaning is approximately 50% higher.
  • Commercial spaces (offices, shops): custom quote based on scope and frequency.

For a quick estimate, use the free quote on the main page or contact us – we'll send you a custom offer.

How to Order

  1. Fill out the form for a free quote (cleaning type, area, frequency) or write us by email / call us.
  2. We'll send you a price quote and suggest a date.
  3. After your approval, we'll perform the cleaning. Payment by transfer or cash by agreement, typically within 7 days after service.
